Quiz Flip Cards

Question 1:

What is SIR (Special Intensive Revision) of Electoral Rolls?

Solution 1:

A legally-backed process initiated by the ECI for house-to-house voter verification to ensure only eligible citizens are included and duplicates/ineligibles are removed.

Question 2:

Under which constitutional and statutory provisions is SIR conducted??

Solution 2:

Article 324 – Powers of Election Commission Section 21 of RP Act, 1950 – Special Revision of Electoral Rolls

Question 3:

What is the qualifying date for SIR 2025 in Bihar?"

Solution 3:

July 1, 2025

Question 4:

Who conducts door-to-door verification during SIR

Solution 4:

Booth Level Officers (BLOs)
